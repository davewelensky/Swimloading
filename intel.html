False Bay Crossing — Key Requirements
🌡️
Water temp ≥ 18°C (skins record attempt)
Satellite SST (20 Feb 2026): Miller's Point ~17°C · Open bay mid-crossing ~19°C · Strand/Rooi Els end ~20°C. Thermal gradient across the route — cooler start, warmer finish. Bay warming trend confirmed. Community readings (19 Feb): Miller's Point 17°C · Simons Town 15°C · Glencairn 16.5°C.

🌊 Channel prep swimmers: At 16–17°C at the start, False Bay exactly matches peak English Channel conditions (late July/August). Temperature is no barrier — this is the target environment.
💨
NW wind
NW wind drives an anti-clockwise bay circulation, pushing current eastward — directly assisting the W→E crossing. SE wind reverses this. Wind is the dominant driver of False Bay surface currents; tides are weak and secondary. Check Sunday forecast on the Wind tab.
🌊
Calm to moderate swell
Big swell adds significant time. Good or Bumpy conditions both viable — Choppy is marginal.
🏊 False Bay = English Channel Distance
33km · 16–17°C · open ocean. The only accessible Channel-equivalent crossing in the Southern Hemisphere. See the Prediction tab for Channel-prep benchmarks.
