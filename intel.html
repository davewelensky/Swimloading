False Bay Crossing — Key Requirements
🌡️
Water temp ≥ 18°C (skins record attempt)
Thermal gradient across the route — cooler start (Miller's Point end), warmer finish (Strand/Rooi Els end). Check live community logs on the NOW tab for current readings.

🌊 Channel prep swimmers: False Bay water matches peak English Channel conditions (late July/August: 16–17°C). Temperature is no barrier — this is the target environment.
💨
NW wind
NW wind drives an anti-clockwise bay circulation, pushing current eastward — directly assisting the W→E crossing. SE wind reverses this. Wind is the dominant driver of False Bay surface currents; tides are weak and secondary. Check the 7-day forecast on the Window tab.
🌊
Calm to moderate swell
Big swell adds significant time. Good or Bumpy conditions both viable — Choppy is marginal.
🏊 False Bay = English Channel Distance
33km · 16–17°C · open ocean. The only accessible Channel-equivalent crossing in the Southern Hemisphere. See the Prediction tab for Channel-prep benchmarks.
